Graduate Research Assistant (Oct. 2023 – May 2024)
I was a primary analyst for project investigating biomarkers in the NIA-funded Health and Retirement Study (HRS), with a focus on Dementia and Alzheimer’s disease. I Conducted comprehensive analyses of plasma biomarkers, developed group classifications based on biomarker elevations, and performed differential gene expression analysis tied to cognitive decline. I built statistical models to examine relationships between cognitive scores and outputs from OLINK and Quanterix platforms and explored the validation of a previously published aging clock model from ARIC study data using HRS datasets.
